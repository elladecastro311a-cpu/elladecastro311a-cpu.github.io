Photography

Travel, landscape, and editorial selects.

Curated collections that prioritize place, light, and clarity. Each series will be tightened to strong selects (typically 12–20 images) to match how internship reviewers evaluate portfolios.

Morocco — Sahara Italy — Mille Miglia Portugal — Streets Sanibel — Beach Tallahassee — Snow

Morocco — Sahara Desert & Aït Ben Haddou

A visual essay moving from the vastness of the Sahara Desert into the earthen architecture of Aït Ben Haddou, tracing movement and human presence across southern Morocco.

Italy — Mille Miglia

The Mille Miglia is a historic Italian road race, now run as a classic car rally, celebrating vehicles built before 1957 as they retrace the original Brescia to Rome route through Italy.

Albufeira, Portugal

Coastal landscapes and everyday life in southern Portugal— beaches, marinas, pastel architecture, and quiet moments along the Algarve.

Sanibel — Beach

Quiet coastal moments focused on light, people, and stillness along the shoreline.

Tallahassee — Snow Day

A rare snowfall in Tallahassee transforms familiar paths, parks, and buildings into something quiet, unexpected, and almost unreal.
